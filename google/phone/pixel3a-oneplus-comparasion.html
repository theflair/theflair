Pixel 3A might scare One Plus 6T

Generally most of the mid-range smartphones are lacking in camera, screen quality and software updates. This time Google had made a different approach with it's Pixel 3A and 3A XL having a gorgeous OLED screen, flawless Pixel Launcher UI and the same camera module present in the flagship smartphone Pixel 3.

Google has recently enveiled the pricing of Pixel 3A and Pixel 3A XL. The pricing of the smaller brother Pixel 3A is $399 and the bigger brother Pixel 3A XL is quoted with $479 in the United States. It is one of the unique mid-range smartphone in the US.

But in India, with the base price of ₹40,000(around $570) it is no way a part of mid-range smartphones. According to the price category ranges in India, a phone with a price tag of 40,000 INR is treated as the entry level Premium class smartphone.

Although having Pixe 3A is having price complexities in the Indian Market, with all the premium specs it is a pretty worthy competitor of One Plus 6T.

One of the weakest points of One Plus 6T is the camera, which is the strongest area of the Pixel 3A.

Side by side spec comparision of Pixel 3A and One Plus 6T

Features Pixel 3A One Plus 6T
Screen OLED AmoLED
Processor SD 670 SD 845
RAM 4 GB 8 GB
UI Pixel Launcher Oxygen OS
Camera 12.2MP(OIS + EIS) 16MP(OIS) + 20MP
Updates 3 years(latest) --
Price ₹39,999 ₹39,999

Although the Pixel 3A has a downside at the Processor and RAM, it has few greater flagship range specs like the marvelous Camera,OLED screen, 3 years of Updates and many more Premium features to attract few of the One Plus Customers which affects its sales.

There might be a part of Customers who has Camera as the highest priority and cannot afford the flagship Google Pixel device. Such customers who can afford a device 0f ₹40,000, now has a great device of their choice. And the only device that is present at this price is One Plus 6T.

So, there might be a slighter change in the sales of One Plus because of Pixel 3A.
